The thesis? A rising tide lifts all boats.

The mechanism? Radical transparency.


At Nucleus, we believe the most powerful asset a physician can have is information. By freely and openly hosting information for all to access, our goal is to ensure physicians make informed career decisions. The end goal of doing this? More competitive career opportunities for all.

Nucleus provides you with job opportunities across all medical specialties, from moonlighting shifts to full-time employment, opportunities for residents through attendings. But that's not enough ‚Äì you should be able to assess if the offers you're considering are competitive. With compensation data sourced from members like you, you're able to assess how your offer stacks up and if it's worth pursuing.

For those earlier in their career, potentially deciding their residency rank list or where to accept their first job, there are residency program and job review sections that allow users to share their experiences at their residency and prior practices. Historically, this information has been siloed and gatekept, but we hope that through transparency users can use this data to make informed decisions.

This platform will only be as useful as its userbase, and I hope you will feel compelled to contribute to it by submitting job reviews, residency reviews, compensation data, or just by having a good time in the forum with fellow physicians.


To new friends and the betterment of the field of medicine.
